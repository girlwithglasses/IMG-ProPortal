Average Nucleotide Identity (ANI)

Average Nucleotide Identity (ANI) is a measure of nucleotide-level genomic similarity between the coding regions of two genomes. First, BBHs between a genome pair are computed as pairwise bidirectional best nSimScan hits of genes having 70% or more identity and at least 70% coverage of the shorter gene.
