Dear instructors & students of bioinformatics & microbial genome analysis courses and/or curious users:
Please use IMG/EDU which does not require login. IMG/EDU contains publicly available genomes and its content is updated twice/year.
You need an account to login into IMG only if you need access to the most up to date genome datasets or to metagenome datasets.
You need an account to login into IMG/ER only if you need access to private (non public) genome or metagenome datasets

Starting May 2015 use of IMG accounts are being deprecated and its being replaced with JGI Single Sign On (SSO).
We are encouraging all users to sign up for a JGI SSO account to start the account migration process.
As of Jan. 1, 2016 all IMG accounts will be disabled and only JGI SSO accounts can be used with IMG.
