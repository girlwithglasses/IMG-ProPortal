About IMG/ProPortal

The marine cyanobacterium Prochlorococcus, which is abundant in the oceans, is a key model system in microbial ecology. IMG/ProMod provides Prochlorococcus and its closely related Synechococcus and Cyanophage genomes integrated with a comprehensive set of publicly available isolate and single cell genomes, and a rich set of publicly available metagenome samples. IMG/ProMod includes genomic, transcriptomic, metagenomic and population data from both cultivated strains and wild populations of cyanobacteria and phage.
IMG/ProPortal relies on IMG's data warehouse and comparative analysis tools (Nucleic Acids Research, Volume 42 Issue D1) and is a descendant of ProPortal (Nucleic Acids Research Volume 40 Issue D1).
